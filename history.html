Cyberpunk Sub-culture History


The sub-culture of cyberpunk has been one at the origin of the cyberpunk genre when writiers and creators were exeprimenting with new ways to tell stories. One of the creators was Mike Pondsmith the creator of Cyberpunk 2020 and his own version of cyberpunk. Mike Pondsmith created Cyberpunk

Mike Pondsmith made the first of his Cyberpunk games with in 1988 with R. Talsorian Games and this was his first attempt to get dark and gritty with the story-telling of the world he was going to publish.

The 1990s was the first debut of Cyberpunk 2020 and then it was simply another table-top RPG competeing among other table-top games such as Dungeons and Dragons,and Vampire Bloodlines. What made Cyberpunk sitck out was its unique take on humans and their interaction and merging with technology. It brought about a different kind of story telling within the Cyberpunk genre and sci-fi, while also building off of previous sci-fi themes.

Since then Cyberpunk has remained popular within its own niche communtities but, recenetly the title of Cyberpunk has gained more popularity with the release date of the videogame Cyberpunk 2077 which an culmination of the events of what happend in Cyberpunk 2020 the table-top game.

After the release of Cyberpunk 2020 another table-top game will be released with the title of Cyberpunk Red detailing the events that would happend between2045 and 2077 as the Cybeprunks character will go though the world in the aftermath of the Third Corporate War.
