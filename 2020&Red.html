Table-top RPGs was the first way the world of Cyberpunk was introduced to its now current fans and even now the game itself has a lot of replayability and content that still remains fresh.

Cyberpunk 2020 offered something that fans of the original Cyberpunk table-top were looking for and that was anexpansion on the leading events that caused the world to spiral out of control after the Third Corporate War.

As an major event for cyberpunk fans,2020 continuned with the stoy of cyberpunk with great effects and even more world building which is why it is still played today by even casual fans of Cyberpunk.

On top of continuing the over arching story of cyberpunk, 2020 had a lot to offer in terms of replayability and less explored story options.
