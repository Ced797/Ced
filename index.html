Cyberpunk is a vast genre full of different interpreations, and re-imaginings. However one has gained popularity and that is the Cyberpunk created by Mike Pondsmith.

Cyberpunk has always had a following of people who releated to the source material of the Cyberpunk genre created by Mike Pondsmith. These people are known as Cyberpunks. Although there are multiple forms of cyberpunk Mike Pondsmith's Cyberpunk has been distinguished amongst the rest because of its popularity and orgins.

Cyberpunks vary just like the overall genre and multiple people are into this interation of cyberpunk but, cyberpunk has become popular in areas such as Poland, Russian and Finland. Cyberpunk has also attracted the general punk genre of people becasue of its punk origins, anit-establishment themes,examination of the impact of drug culture, technology, and the sexual revolution while avoiding the utopian tendencies of earlier science fiction themes.
